Recharger

The recharger is a machine in Potentia that is used to charge Power Tools. It will draw power from connected generators only if the recharger is powered by redstone. The recharger will charge tools with or without a redstone signal. In order to connect the recharger to a generator, it must either be directly touching the generator, or connected via wires. It will draw power at a rate of 5 Machine Energy per tick. The recharger has a maximum capacity of 25,000 machine energy. To view the amount of power in the recharger, you must sneak-right-click the recharger. To add a tool to be charged, you must simply right-click the recharger with the desired power tool in hand. This will add the tool into the inventory of the recharger. To view the inventory of the recharger, simply right-click on it. The tool will charge at a rate of 1 Tool Energy per tick. 1 Tool Energy is the equivalent of 5 Machine Energy. The generator generates Machine Energy, not Tool Energy. To remove the tool from the recharger, open the inventory of the recharger and then click on the tool you wish to extract.
