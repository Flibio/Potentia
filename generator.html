Generator

The generator is the first item you are going to want to craft in Potentia. The generator generates power using coal that you supply it as long as it is powered by a redstone signal. It will generate 25 Machine Energy per tick while using 1 piece of coal every 2.5 seconds. Right-clicking the generator will display the current amount of fuel and the current power stored inside. The generator has a maximum capacity of 1,000,000 Machine Energy. The generator is compatible with Displays.
